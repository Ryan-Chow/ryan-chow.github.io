Traditional Tutors Haven't Caught Up.

The SAT/PSAT went fully digital, and it's a different test: adaptive sections, a built-in graphing calculator, shorter reading passages, and a different pacing strategy than the old paper exam. Most established tutoring services are staffed by adults who only ever took the old paper SAT, years or decades ago. Every tutor at Peer Prep Academy has personally taken the current digital SAT — so we're teaching the exam students actually face, not the one they remember from years back.
